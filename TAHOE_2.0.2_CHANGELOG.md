# Adventure 2 v2.0.2 — Trail Climb

v2.0.2 turns the Tahoe preview into a genuine playable challenge.

## New gameplay

- Five moving Pine Beetles placed across the first mountain trail
- Stomp combat: land on a beetle from above to defeat it
- Three-heart health system
- Damage knockback and brief invulnerability
- Respawn after losing all hearts
- Granite Overlook checkpoint
- Beetle counter in the HUD
- Trailhead remains blocked until all five beetles are defeated
- Updated objectives throughout the climb
- Trail Climb completion screen

## Play flow

1. Enter the portal and watch or skip The Arrival.
2. Explore the shoreline.
3. Climb the granite trail.
4. Stomp all five Pine Beetles.
5. Activate the Granite Overlook checkpoint.
6. Reach the upper trailhead.

Adventure 1 remains untouched and Tahoe still launches directly from the main title screen.
