# v2.2.0 — Superman Combat

## Combat overhaul
- Mario-style beetle stomps with a strong rebound.
- Hold and release Rainbow Power for three charge levels.
- Full charge activates the large Superman Blast.
- Blast piercing at higher charge levels.
- Combo multiplier, score, floating combat text, hit-stop, camera shake, and screen flash.
- Dust, leaf, damage, and rainbow particle effects.
- Heart and instant-power drops.
- Walking, charging, flying, and armored beetles.
- Armored beetles resist stomps and require Rainbow Power.
- Improved jump buffering, coyote time, and variable jump height.
- Tahoe route expanded from 4,600 to 6,200 world units with 12 beetles.

Adventure 1 remains untouched at v1.0.10.
