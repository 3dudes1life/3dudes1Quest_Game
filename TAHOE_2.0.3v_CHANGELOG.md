# Adventure 2 v2.0.3v — First Playable

This is the handoff build intended for William to upload and play before more systems are added.

## Playability polish
- Five hearts for a more forgiving first test
- Coyote time lets a jump still register just after leaving an edge
- Jump buffering remembers a jump pressed just before landing
- Variable jump gravity provides better short and long jumps
- Smoother aerial control and deceleration
- Wider platform landing tolerance
- More forgiving Pine Beetle stomp detection
- Clear temporary play-instruction banner
- Improved camera pullback and story reveal at the overlook

## Developer shortcuts
No visible developer shortcuts are included in this public build. Future shortcuts can be locked behind a URL flag or secret activation sequence.
