# Tahoe 2.4.0 — Rise of the Pine Beetle Queen

## Phase 3: Boss Adventure
- Four destructible beetle nests with visible health
- Nests hatch chargers, flyers, and armored beetles until destroyed
- Dynamic Tahoe weather: sun, clouds, rain, storm, and victory rainbow
- Rigsby barks near secret crystals and growls near nests
- Pine Beetle Queen with three combat phases
- Queen charge attacks, flight, minion spawning, and corruption bombs
- Dedicated boss health and phase HUD
- Superman Blast deals bonus damage to nests and the Queen
- Stomp attacks work against the Queen when timed from above
- Forest restoration and rainbow finale after victory
- Alaska portal moved beyond the Queen's summit arena
- Save data includes nest and boss progress
- Adventure 1 remains untouched
